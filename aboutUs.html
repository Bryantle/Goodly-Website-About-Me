We share our plans openly so that all of us understand the concrete steps we can take to build a better future. Each project listed on the GoodlyLabs website includes a progress bar reporting its status and a page at the Open Science Framework allowing researchers to join our efforts. We invite anyone to participate in our work – as a volunteer, research collaborator, sponsor, or social media fan.

The 3-Step Learning Process
1
Acknowledge

Goodly is using a mass-collaborative research software so that we can investigating questions, including: - How can we develop a collective immunity to faulty information? - What information in a news article is credible and what portions seem mistaken or poorly supported? - How do different societies (in different places and at different times) promote different notions of race and gender? - How do peoples’ grievances get expressed through protest - How can governments and civil society address those grievances more peacefully and effectively?

2
Demonstrate

Goodly will organize, publicize, and film a collective “GlobalWave,” where people from around the world come into the streets and demonstrate that we can act in concert as a global community. This historic event will require little from individual participants, but deeply reshape the consciousness of humanity as we prove to ourselves that properly used Internet technologies give us the capacity to act as a community without needing the initiative or permission of our governments or other traditional institutions. We can do what we want without needing to change the old way of doing things.

1
Rebuild & Grow

Goodly will deploy SamePage, a free software that consists of a set of online collective decision-making tools that communities of all sizes will be able to use to set priorities, research problem solutions, and deliberate about tradeoffs and courses of action. Over the next five years, the Goodly community will communicate using both social and traditional media and seek partnerships with video production and distribution companies to ensure that our work, findings, process, mission, and values are accessible in the world’s most popular and viral medium. Goodly’s tools and approaches will be widely available to any community seeking to consciously improve individual and collective welfare.
